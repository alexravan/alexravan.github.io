idikia

During my internship at Cogo Labs, along with 4 other interns, I worked on idikia. idikia is a politically-themed news and entertainment platform, similar to Buzzfeed. It includes a political search engine, trending articles, videos with Natural Language analysis, quizzes, listicles, and a game. It was developed with:

  • AngularJS
  • Flask & Python
  • MySQL
  • C3.js
  • Bootstrap
Video Analysis

To use idikia's proprietary video analysis feature, all you need is a link to a YouTube video that features closed captioning. Just copy the link, paste it in the idikia search bar, and hit Search.

The natural language processing algorithm will then reveal some interesting facts and figures about the video. These inlcude the most used words in the video, repeated one-, two-, three-, and four-word phrases, lexical diversity, as well as common bi- and tri-grams.

Quizzes

The quizzes featured on idikia were done in the style of Buzzfeed and ClickHole. Through a series of questions, users were able to get the results of quizzes such as “Which party do your views align with?” or “Which historical US president are you most like?”

The quiz module was built entirely from scratch using Angular. It was also one of the most popular features that received lots of reengagment from our users.
